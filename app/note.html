A Note From Me

I will wait for you
No pressure. No rush. I’ll be here when you’re ready.

I know you're hurt, and I know I'm the reason. I’m not writing this to pull you into a conversation or force anything from you. I just want to say things clearly, without ego, without overthinking.

I care about you — more than I express sometimes, and more than I show on the surface. I don’t always get everything right, and I won’t pretend otherwise. But I’m trying to understand things better instead of reacting fast or assuming.

You don’t owe me a reply right now. You don’t have to forgive instantly or act like nothing happened. Take your time. Whenever you’re ready — even if that’s later — I’ll listen properly. With patience. No excuses.

— Maheshwaran
