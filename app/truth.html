Things I Want To Say Properly

  • I never meant to hurt you — even if I did. I’m not ignoring what you felt.
  • I react fast sometimes, but when it comes to you… I actually feel things deeply. That’s why I’m trying to grow instead of just defending myself.
  • You don’t need to be okay immediately. Your feelings are valid, and I respect the space you need.
  • If something matters to you, then it matters to me too — even if I understand it late.
  • I don’t want to lose the connection we have. I’m open to fixing things slowly, calmly, and the right way.
